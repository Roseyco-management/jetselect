# Phase 11 Plan 1: Amsterdam City Landing Page

**Create high-impact Amsterdam landing page targeting "privéjet Amsterdam" with local SEO optimization**

## Objective

Build the highest-priority city landing page for Amsterdam, the largest private jet market in the Netherlands. This page will:
- Target "privéjet Amsterdam" keyword (highest volume city search)
- Provide Amsterdam-specific private jet information (airports, FBOs, regulations, pricing)
- Serve as hub for Amsterdam sub-pages (buying guide, charter services, routes)
- Capture local search traffic and convert high-net-worth Amsterdam residents/businesses

## Target Metrics

- **Keyword:** privéjet Amsterdam (P1 priority)
- **Word Count:** 1,800-2,000 words
- **Schema.org:** BreadcrumbList + FAQPage (8-10 Amsterdam-specific questions)
- **Internal Links:** Link to main pillar pages + Amsterdam sub-pages
- **Featured Snippet Target:** Amsterdam airport comparison table

## Content Structure

### Hero Section
- Amsterdam skyline with private jet imagery
- H1: "Privéjet in Amsterdam - Complete Gids voor Schiphol & Lelystad"
- Subheading: "De ultieme gids voor privéjet services in Amsterdam en omgeving"

### Main Sections (H2)

1. **Introductie: Amsterdam als Privéjet Hub**
   - Amsterdam's position as Netherlands' primary business aviation center
   - Proximity to financial district (Zuidas), major corporations, international business
   - Statistics: private jet traffic volume, business aviation growth

2. **Schiphol Airport - Private Jet Terminals**
   - Schiphol Privium terminal overview
   - Jet Centre terminal (General Aviation Terminal East)
   - FBO services, facilities, customs/immigration
   - Ground handling, fueling, hangar options
   - Slot restrictions and peak time considerations

3. **Lelystad Airport - Business Aviation Alternative**
   - Lelystad as overflow and growing GA hub (30 min from Amsterdam)
   - Lower costs vs Schiphol
   - Facilities and services
   - When to choose Lelystad vs Schiphol

4. **Amsterdam FBO Services & Ground Handling**
   - List of major FBOs: Jet Centre, Air Service Liège Netherlands, PrivatAir, Signature Flight Support
   - Services comparison table: customs/immigration, concierge, car service, catering
   - Pricing ranges for ground handling

5. **Privéjet Kopen in Amsterdam**
   - Amsterdam buyer demographics (finance, tech, real estate, international business)
   - Local aircraft brokers and dealers in Amsterdam region
   - Tax considerations for Amsterdam-based owners (link to business tax guide)
   - Link to main buying guide pillar page

6. **Privéjet Huren in Amsterdam**
   - Charter pricing from Amsterdam (light/midsize/heavy jets)
   - Popular Amsterdam routes: London, Paris, Geneva, Dubai, New York
   - Empty legs opportunities from Schiphol
   - Link to rent vs buy guide

7. **Routes & Destinations from Amsterdam**
   - Top 10 business routes from Amsterdam (with flight times)
   - Comparison table: AMS-LON (1h), AMS-PAR (1.5h), AMS-GVA (1.5h), AMS-DXB (6h), AMS-JFK (8h)
   - Aircraft recommendations by route distance

8. **Kosten & Prijzen - Amsterdam Specifiek**
   - Operating costs for Amsterdam-based aircraft
   - Hangar/storage costs at Schiphol vs Lelystad
   - Amsterdam crew availability and costs
   - Link to main cost guide pillar

9. **Regelgeving & Vergunningen**
   - EASA regulations for aircraft based in Amsterdam
   - Dutch CAA (ILT) registration process
   - Noise restrictions at Schiphol (impact on operations)
   - Environmental regulations (EU ETS, CORSIA)

10. **Waarom Amsterdam Kiezen**
    - Amsterdam's advantages: central European location, world-class airport infrastructure
    - Business ecosystem (headquarters of major corporations)
    - Lifestyle factors (culture, restaurants, hotels for clients/crew)

11. **FAQ - Amsterdam Privéjet**

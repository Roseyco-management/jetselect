### Main Sections (H2)

1. **Introductie: Schiphol als Europa's Business Aviation Hub**
   - Schiphol statistics: Europe's 3rd busiest airport, 15,000+ business aviation movements/year
   - Strategic location: Amsterdam 15 km, Rotterdam 50 km, accessible to entire Randstad
   - Infrastructure: 6 runways, 24/7 operations, all aircraft types supported
   - Why Schiphol dominates Netherlands private jet market

2. **Schiphol Private Jet Terminals**

   **General Aviation Terminal East (GAT East):**
   - Location: East side of airport (separate from commercial terminals)
   - Facilities: 2,500m² terminal, VIP lounges, meeting rooms, crew facilities
   - Services: customs/immigration, security screening, concierge
   - Capacity: 150+ aircraft movements per day
   - Access: 10 min drive from main commercial terminal, 20 min from Amsterdam center

   **Schiphol Privium (JetCenter):**
   - Premium VIP terminal for commercial first-class and private jet passengers
   - Location: Near Pier D (integrated with commercial airport)
   - Fast-track security and customs
   - Luxury lounges with Dutch design aesthetics
   - Car service to aircraft (no walking through public terminals)

3. **Schiphol FBO Services - Complete Vergelijking**

   Detailed comparison table (featured snippet target):

   **Jet Centre Schiphol:**
   - Services: ground handling, fueling, hangar, de-icing, catering, customs
   - Fleet: all aircraft types (light jets to Gulfstream G650/Global 7500)
   - Hangar: yes, heated hangars available
   - Pricing: €€€ (premium)
   - Best for: ultra-long-range flights, large cabin aircraft

   **Air Service Liège Netherlands:**
   - Services: ground handling, fueling, basic catering
   - Fleet: light to midsize jets
   - Hangar: limited availability
   - Pricing: €€ (mid-range)
   - Best for: European flights, midsize aircraft

   **Signature Flight Support Schiphol:**
   - Services: full-service FBO (ground handling, fueling, concierge, crew lounge)
   - Fleet: all types (part of global FBO network)
   - Hangar: yes
   - Pricing: €€€ (premium)
   - Best for: international travelers, repeat customers (Signature network)

   **PrivatAir Handling:**
   - Services: specialized VIP handling, charter operations support
   - Fleet: light to heavy jets
   - Hangar: no (apron parking)
   - Pricing: €€€ (VIP-focused)
   - Best for: heads of state, celebrity clients, ultra-discreet service

4. **Schiphol Ground Services - Wat is Inbegrepen?**
   - Ground handling: towing, marshalling, GPU, air start units
   - Fueling: Jet-A1 available 24/7, fuel prices competitive with EU average
   - De-icing: winter operations (Nov-Mar), de-icing bays near GAT East
   - Catering: 5+ catering providers (kosher, halal, vegan options available)
   - Customs/Immigration: Schengen and non-Schengen processing
   - Security: TSA-equivalent screening for US-bound flights
   - Crew facilities: lounges, showers, sleep rooms, briefing rooms

5. **Schiphol Hangar & Storage**
   - Hangar availability: 50+ private hangars (various sizes)
   - Pricing: €3,000-€8,000/month (light jets), €8,000-€15,000/month (heavy jets)
   - Heated vs unheated hangars
   - Long-term vs short-term storage
   - Maintenance facilities on-site (approved service centers)

6. **Slot Management & Peak Times**
   - Schiphol slot coordination system
   - Peak hours: 07:00-10:00, 17:00-20:00 (high commercial traffic)
   - Business aviation priority windows
   - Advance booking requirements (24-48 hours for peak times)
   - Flexibility for emergency/diplomatic flights

7. **Kosten & Prijzen - Schiphol Specifiek**
   - Landing fees: €500-€2,500 (by aircraft MTOW)
   - Parking fees: €100-€500 per day (apron), hangar costs higher
   - Ground handling: €800-€2,500 per turnaround
   - Fueling: market rate + 10-15% markup
   - Total cost examples by aircraft type:
     - Light jet (Citation CJ3): €2,000-€3,000 per visit
     - Midsize jet (Challenger 350): €3,500-€5,000 per visit
     - Heavy jet (Gulfstream G650): €6,000-€10,000 per visit

8. **Routes & Destinations from Schiphol**
   - Top 10 routes by volume: London, Paris, Geneva, Dubai, New York, Moscow, Miami, Nice, Istanbul, Zurich
   - Flight time comparison table
   - Aircraft recommendations by route distance
   - Link to Amsterdam-London route page (Plan 11-05)

9. **Regelgeving & Operaties**
   - EASA Part-NCO and Part-NCC operations
   - Dutch CAA (ILT) oversight
   - Noise restrictions: QC4 limit (equivalent to 97 EPNdB), night flight quotas
   - Environmental regulations: EU ETS (carbon emissions trading)
   - PPR (Prior Permission Required) for certain aircraft types/times

10. **Waarom Schiphol Kiezen?**
    - **Advantages:**
      - Europe's best-connected airport (direct flights to 330+ destinations)
      - 24/7 operations (no curfew unlike many European airports)
      - All aircraft types supported (including A380, 747-8 - relevant for VIP conversions)
      - Premium FBO services (4+ options)
      - Maintenance facilities on-site (convenient for owners)
      - Central European location (2-hour flight radius covers 500M people)
    - **Disadvantages:**
      - Higher costs vs Rotterdam/Lelystad (20-30% premium)
      - Slot congestion during peak times
      - Complex taxi procedures (large airport)

11. **Schiphol vs Andere Nederlandse Luchthavens**
    - Comparison table: Schiphol vs Rotterdam The Hague vs Lelystad
    - When to choose Schiphol: intercontinental flights, large aircraft, premium services
    - When to choose alternatives: European flights, smaller aircraft, cost savings

3. **Luchthavens - Amsterdam naar London**

   **Departure Options (Amsterdam):**
   - Amsterdam Schiphol (EHAM) - Primary choice
     - Distance from Amsterdam center: 15 km (15 min)
     - FBO services: Jet Centre, Signature, Air Service Liège
     - Advantage: central location, premium facilities
   - Lelystad Airport (EHLE) - Cost-effective alternative
     - Distance from Amsterdam: 45 km (30 min)
     - Lower costs (20-30% vs Schiphol)
     - Advantage: no slot restrictions, quieter

   **Arrival Options (London):**
   - London Biggin Hill (EGKB) - Most popular for business
     - Distance to City of London: 22 km (30-40 min)
     - No slot restrictions, fast turnaround
     - FBO: Biggin Hill Airport Limited
   - London Luton (EGGW) - Mid-range option
     - Distance to central London: 56 km (45-60 min)
     - Good connectivity, moderate costs
   - London Farnborough (EGLF) - Premium option
     - Distance to central London: 60 km (50-70 min)
     - TAG Farnborough (luxury FBO), VIP services
     - Higher costs but premium experience
   - London City Airport (EGLC) - Closest to City
     - Distance to City of London: 11 km (15 min)
     - Short runway (light jets only)
     - Slot restrictions, higher costs
   - RAF Northolt (EGWU) - Government/VIP
     - Distance to central London: 19 km (25-35 min)
     - Restricted (government/diplomatic flights only)

   **Midsize Jets (7-9 passengers):**
   - Best for: business teams, family travel, more luggage

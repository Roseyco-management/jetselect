# Phase 11 Plan 2: Rotterdam City Landing Page

**Create Rotterdam landing page targeting "privéjet Rotterdam" with focus on Rotterdam The Hague Airport**

## Objective

Build the second-priority city landing page for Rotterdam, the Netherlands' second-largest city and major port/logistics hub. This page will:
- Target "privéjet Rotterdam" keyword (P2 priority)
- Focus on Rotterdam The Hague Airport (RTM) as primary private jet gateway
- Highlight Rotterdam's business aviation advantages (port proximity, less congestion than Schiphol)
- Capture Rotterdam business travelers and logistics executives

## Target Metrics

- **Keyword:** privéjet Rotterdam (P2 priority)
- **Word Count:** 1,600-1,800 words
- **Schema.org:** BreadcrumbList + FAQPage (8 Rotterdam-specific questions)
- **Internal Links:** Link to Amsterdam page, main pillar pages, RTM airport page
- **Featured Snippet Target:** Rotterdam vs Amsterdam airport comparison

## Content Structure

### Hero Section
- Rotterdam skyline with Erasmus Bridge and private jet imagery
- H1: "Privéjet Rotterdam - Rotterdam The Hague Airport & Services"
- Subheading: "Privéjet services in Rotterdam: snelle toegang tot Europe's grootste haven"

### Main Sections (H2)

1. **Introductie: Rotterdam als Business Aviation Hub**
   - Rotterdam's position as port/logistics center (world's 10th largest port)
   - Business demographics: maritime, logistics, oil & gas, chemicals
   - Growth of business aviation at Rotterdam The Hague Airport

2. **Rotterdam The Hague Airport (RTM)**
   - Overview: 6 km from city center (closer than Schiphol to Amsterdam)
   - General Aviation Terminal facilities
   - FBO services: Atlantic Aviation, Signature Flight Support
   - Advantages: less congestion, faster turnaround, lower costs vs Schiphol
   - Runway capabilities and aircraft size limitations

3. **Rotterdam FBO Services**
   - Atlantic Aviation Rotterdam (primary FBO)
   - Services: customs/immigration, ground handling, fueling, hangar
   - Pricing comparison vs Schiphol
   - Concierge services, car service to city center

4. **Privéjet Kopen in Rotterdam**
   - Rotterdam buyer demographics (maritime executives, logistics companies)
   - Aircraft brokers serving Rotterdam region
   - Tax considerations for Rotterdam-based owners
   - Link to main buying guide

5. **Privéjet Huren in Rotterdam**
   - Charter pricing from Rotterdam (light/midsize jets primarily)
   - Popular routes: London, Brussels, Frankfurt, Oslo, Aberdeen (energy sector)
   - Empty legs from RTM
   - Link to rent vs buy guide

6. **Routes & Destinations from Rotterdam**
   - Top routes: London (1h), Brussels (45min), Frankfurt (1h), Aberdeen (1.5h)
   - Comparison table with flight times
   - Aircraft recommendations (light/midsize jets optimal for RTM routes)

7. **Kosten & Prijzen - Rotterdam Specifiek**
   - Operating costs for Rotterdam-based aircraft
   - RTM airport fees vs Schiphol (20-30% lower)
   - Hangar availability and costs
   - Crew costs (similar to Amsterdam region)

8. **Rotterdam vs Amsterdam - Welke Kiezen?**
   - Comparison table: location, costs, facilities, congestion, aircraft size limits
   - When to choose RTM: port business, shorter European routes, lower costs
   - When to choose Schiphol: long-haul flights, larger aircraft, premium facilities

9. **Regelgeving & Operaties**
   - EASA regulations (same as Amsterdam)
   - RTM operational considerations (runway length, noise restrictions)
   - Ground handling requirements

10. **FAQ - Rotterdam Privéjet**

## Key Information Highlights

### Route Specifics
- **Distance:** 358 km (222 miles)
- **Flight time:** 55-75 minutes depending on aircraft type
- **Door-to-door time:** 2h 05min (private) vs 4h 15min - 5h 15min (commercial)
- **Time savings:** 2-3 hours per single trip

### Airport Details
**Amsterdam Departure:**
- Schiphol (EHAM) - Primary choice, 15 km from center
- Lelystad (EHLE) - Cost-effective alternative, 45 km from center, 20-30% cheaper

**London Arrival:**
- Biggin Hill (EGKB) - Most popular for business, 22 km to City of London
- London City (EGLC) - Closest (11 km), light jets only
- Farnborough (EGLF) - Premium luxury, TAG FBO
- Luton (EGGW) - Mid-range option, 56 km from center

### Pricing Summary
- Light jets: €7,000-€10,000 one-way
- Midsize jets: €11,000-€15,000 one-way (recommended for most travelers)
- Super midsize: €15,000-€20,000 one-way
- Heavy jets: €22,000-€30,000 one-way
- Empty legs: 30-50% discount (high availability on this route)

### Business Use Cases
- Finance: Zuidas ↔ City of London
- Tech: Amsterdam tech scene ↔ London Tech City
- Consulting: Big 4 partner travel
- Legal: Cross-border M&A, litigation
- Real estate: Property investors
- Day trip feasibility: Depart 07:30, full day meetings, return 19:30

### Regulatory Requirements (Post-Brexit)
- Valid passport required (ID card no longer sufficient)
- No visa for Dutch nationals (up to 6 months)
- Customs/immigration at both ends
- Goods >€10,000 must be declared
- PETS scheme for pet travel (microchip, rabies vaccination, health certificate)
